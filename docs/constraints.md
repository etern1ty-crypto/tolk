# Product Constraints & Ideology (Tolk)

## Non-Goals (What we are NOT building)
- **Not a MAX clone**: We are not copying MAX or trying to be a state-mandated messenger. Tolk is chosen voluntarily (anti-MAX positioning).
- **Not a Telegram clone**: We are not trying to match Telegram feature-for-feature. We are focusing on users tired of the "super-app" noise and cognitive overload.
- **Not a social network / No algorithmic feed**: No algorithmic feed, no stories just for the sake of having them. No red badges and infinite scroll to drive engagement artificially. No "intimacy" frame.
- **No bloatware**: We reject the "5 tabs at the bottom" (tab bar ×5) design. No heavy animations that drain the battery. Minimal chrome.

## Core Tone and Brand Identity
- **Tone**: Direct, dry, and with character. Not corporate politeness, no "government service" feel. Fast and straight to the point.
- **Cultural Code (RU)**: Built for the RU-community. Stickers, voice messages, forwards, and groups are the norm. Hate for slow apps, ads in chats, and "forced" features.
- **Aesthetics**: Absolute minimalism. Less UI decisions on the screen = faster actions.
- **Privacy**: Normal privacy without marketing hyperbole or paranoia. Honest E2EE (post-MVP).

## The "Tolk" Wedge (Our True Utility)
- **The Chat Wall (Стена чата)**: A dedicated shelf for media and pinned items of *this* dialog. Media won't drown in the chat history.
- **Echoes**: "Look at this later" messages without the screaming push notifications.
- **Simplicity**: No bottom tabs. Spatial UI (List ↔ Chat ↔ Wall). Only core clickables on main screens.
